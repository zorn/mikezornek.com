Hello! My name is Mike Zornek. I describe myself as both a developer and teacher, and I've been building digital products for over 25 years.

From the suburbs of Philadelphia, I code in Elixir for Allovue, which builds accounting software for school districts. In my spare time, I hack on a few different projects, including StudyHall, which hopes to be an open-source courseware for people who teach programming.

When I am not coding, I enjoy watching Phillies baseball and playing video games (mostly laid-back simulations and RPGs).

I am an active member of many Philadelphia-based communities, including: Indy Hall and Philly CocoaHeads.
