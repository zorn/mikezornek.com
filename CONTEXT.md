# mikezornek.com

Personal Hugo site and blog for Mike Zornek. This glossary captures terms
specific to how the site's content is organized and presented.

## Language

**Start Here list**:
A hand-selected, hand-ranked set of blog posts presented as a newcomer's
on-ramp. Its bar is not "my deepest work" but "my most trust-building technical
work": posts that are both approachable and impressive to a first-time visitor,
chosen to earn enough trust that they follow and eventually join the mailing
list. It is a single flat list, not themed collections.
_Avoid_: Featured, Best of, Selected, Favorites, Highlights.

**Start Here entry**:
One item in the Start Here list: a reference to a post plus an optional
editorial note framing why a newcomer should read it.

**Editorial note**:
A short, author-written sentence attached to a Start Here entry that tells the
reader why the post is worth their time or where it fits. Distinct from a post's
own `description` front matter, which is the post's general summary.
